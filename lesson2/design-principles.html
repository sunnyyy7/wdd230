Fitt's Law

Organization/Company: Google

Website Address: Google

Description: Google's mobile search page exemplifies Fitt's Law by having a large search bar as the primary interactive element. The search bar is prominently placed and takes up a significant portion of the screen, making it easy for users to tap and input their search queries quickly.

Rule of Thirds

Organization/Company: National Geographic

Website Address: National Geographic

Description: National Geographic's mobile website often features stunning photography that adheres to the Rule of Thirds. Images are strategically composed with key subjects placed along the imaginary gridlines or at their intersections, resulting in visually balanced and captivating visuals.

White Space and Clean Design

Organization/Company: Apple

Website Address: Apple

Description: Apple's mobile website showcases clean design with generous white space. Each product or feature is given room to breathe, with ample padding and margins between elements. This design approach enhances readability, navigation, and overall user experience by reducing visual noise and emphasizing essential information.
