Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The chosen site name for this chamber of commerce project is Toronto Chamber of Commerce. The url will be https://www.toronto.ca/ This name reflects the locality, Toronto,Cananda, and the purpose of the site, which is to promote local businesses and economic development.

Site Purpose

The Toronto Chamber of Commerce aims to serve as a hub for local businesses, fostering economic growth and community development. The website will provide resources and information for businesses, residents, and visitors, promoting networking opportunities, events, and initiatives that contribute to the prosperity of the region.

Scenarios

A local business owner wants to find information about the city's current economic status. A person is looking for a place to eat. A Dad is looking for things to do for the family. A dad is looking for informaion on sporting events. A visitor is looking for information about good place to visit in Toronto. A local resident is looking on where to buy chinese food.

Color Schema

The color scheme for the Marina Vista Chamber of Commerce website is inspired by the region's coastal landscape.

  • Primary Color: #247BA0 (used for headings and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #70C1B3 (used for backgrounds and highlights)
  • Dark Blue Color: #4472CA
  • Light Blue Color: #CFDEE7
  • Cream Color: #5E7CE2
  • Tan Color: #92B4F4
  • Brown Color: #D0CFCF

Typography

The chosen fonts for the website are:

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
