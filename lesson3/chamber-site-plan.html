Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The chosen site name for this chamber of commerce project is Marina Vista Chamber of Commerce. This name reflects the locality, Marina Vista, and the purpose of the site, which is to promote local businesses and economic development.

Site Purpose

The Marina Vista Chamber of Commerce aims to serve as a hub for local businesses, fostering economic growth and community development. The website will provide resources and information for businesses, residents, and visitors, promoting networking opportunities, events, and initiatives that contribute to the prosperity of the region.

Scenarios

  • What networking events are scheduled this month for local businesses?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What initiatives is the chamber undertaking to support small businesses?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the Marina Vista Chamber of Commerce website is inspired by the region's coastal landscape.

  • Primary Color: #247BA0 (used for headings and accents)
  • Secondary Color: #70C1B3 (used for backgrounds and highlights)

Typography

The chosen fonts for the website are:

  • Heading Font: 'Roboto', sans-serif
  • Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
