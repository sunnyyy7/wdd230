Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Stephen Lynn

Saratoga Springs Utah Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Saratoga Springs Utah Chamber of Commerce site is meant to serve the community of Saratoga Springs, Utah and to highlight the business opportunities and amenities of the area.

Logo

Scenarios

Persona 1

Demographics

"Stan" is a 50-year-old man who has recently started his own business.

Motivation

Stan is motivated by a need to grow his new business to protect his interests.

Stan is looking for ways to engage with other business owners in the area, to get mentorship and advertizing opportunities.

Stan wants to sign up with the chamber of commerce and learn about news and events. He also wants to see who is already a member to see if he knows someone to reach out to.

Stan needs an easy way to connect with the local chamber of commerce.

Persona 2

Demographics

"Cynthia" is a 45-year-old investor.

Motivation

Cynthia is motivated by a desire to succeed.

Cynthia wants to find stable, local companies who want to grow so she can invest in them. She knows the area is full of good opportunities, but she doesn't know where to strat looking.

Cynthia wants to look at the list of businesses who have already joined the chamber of commerce so she can do some research. She also wants to engage with the board of directors about possibly advertising her services.

Cynthia needs to find local companies and engage with local leadership of the chamber.
