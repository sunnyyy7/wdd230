Review the membership benefits below and choose the one that most interests you

NP Membership:

    Recognizing the valuable role non-profit organizations play in our community, the Chamber offers a free membership tier for non-profits.

    Benefits:

  • Connect with like-minded organizations
  • Gain exposure for your cause
  • Potential partnerships with Chamber members to further your mission

Bronze Membership

    The Bronze Membership package is ideal for businesses seeking a solid foundation in the community.

    Benefits:

  • Listing in the Chamber directory
  • Access to member-only discounts
  • Networking events

Silver Membership

    The Silver Membership package builds upon the benefits of Bronze membership and offers additional advantages to help you grow your business.

    Benefits:

  • Everything from the Bronze Membership benefits
  • Featured listing in the Chamber directory
  • Promotion through Chamber social media channels

Gold Membership

    The Gold Membership package is designed for businesses seeking maximum exposure and engagement within the Chamber community.

    Benefits:

  • All benefits of Silver membership
  • Priority listing in the Chamber directory
  • Sponsorship opportunities at Chamber events
  • Exclusive access to business development resources
